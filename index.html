About Me

Front-End Web Developer - Yogi - Doctor of Physical Therapy

Hi there! My name is Kate Crowgey, and I am a front-end web developer, a yogi, and a physical therapist. You might be thinking, "Wow! What an eclectic combination!" I happen to agree. My journey into web development has been non-linear to say the least ...

After college, I worked for a start-up company called Evolent Health as their SharePoint Editor-in-Chief. While I loved my job, my path into physical therapy was already well underway. I went on to earn my doctorate in physical therapy, and later earned a specialization in outpatient orthopedics.

However, as a physical therapist, I felt something was missing. A part of me longed for the creativity and problem-solving that I used as a SharePoint Designer. In my spare time I began to play again with web design, and I immediately rediscovered my passion. I quit my job, and traveled around South America while studying web development.

Additionally, I have been a dedicated student and teacher of Kripalu yoga since 2012. If you're curious to learn more, please feel free visit my website.
